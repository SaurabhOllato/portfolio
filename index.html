my story.

i am a very lazy creative and zealous individual. once i set out on the rigorous path of becoming a chartered accountant back in 2020, i quickly realized that studying wasn't my thing and decided to pursue other passions instead.

one of my biggest joys is editing videos, where my creativity shines, despite my only professional training being a youtube tutorial i watched at 2x speed. people don't know that half of my workflow is trial, error, and a prayer to the "undo" button.

my fascination with video editing began during the 2020 lockdown when i stumbled upon a cool edit online. that moment sparked something within me, and i've been hooked ever since.

outside of my editing suite, i'm an active guy who loves playing football, gaming, and working on my calisthenics skills. surprisingly, i'm also a decent cook and enjoy whipping up meals when the mood strikes.
